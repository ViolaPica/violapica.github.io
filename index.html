I hold a degree in Interior Design and Environmental Architecture and am currently pursuing a Masters in Interior Design at the Royal College of Art — with a specialised focus on revitalising existing structures through innovative renovations that blend functionality and atmosphere.

My strengths lie in robust problem-solving and meticulous attention to design and construction detail. Proficient in Rhino, I also have a passion for freehand drawing and architectural model-making.
